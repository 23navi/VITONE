Still waiting for bus and paying with cash ?

VIT Bus App is a software application developed specifically for VIT students. The main feature of the app is to bring forth an offline payment system with bus tracking facility. The offline payment mode is to ensure a hassle-free transaction between the bus driver and student.

VIT-Stay

VIT Stay App is a simple webservice application built particularly for those students who are willing to stay outside VIT college area. Concept similar to PG rooms. The app facilitates both the owner and the customer. The owners can list their rooms and students can find a room of their requirement in the limited radius and also review their stay cooperative to the new comers.
